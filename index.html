MTN BetaGist

Talk for 6 mins | Get 3 mins extra

MTN BetaGist is a prepaid plan that lets you call anyone on any network in Nigeria at a flat rate of 30 kobo per second.

There's more! For every 6 minutes of calls you make, you get back 3 minutes to keep the conversation going.

How to get on MTN BetaGist

New customers? You're already on it! MTN BetaGist is the default tariff plan for all new SIM registrations.

Existing MTN customer? No worries. You can switch to BetaGist for FREE using any of the channels below:

Text BG to 312

Dial *123*2*1#

Chat with Zigi, MTN's virtual assistant
